I am Venktesh Pandey, currently pursuing my PhD degree in the department of Civil, Architectural, and Environmental Engineering (CAEE) at The University of Texas at Austin. I am specializing in transportation engineering under the supervision of Dr. Stephen Boyles.

I am currently employed as a graduate research assistant. My research interests include traffic assignment models, network optimization, dynamic stochastic control, reinforcement learning, congestion pricing, ridesharing mobility, and stochastic estimation.

My career objective is to develop the understanding of existing transportation technologies, and create tools which can extract their usefulness for the vision of sustainable transportation. I am working towards my disseration topic titled "Dynamic Pricing and Long-term Planning Models for Managed Lanes with Multiple Entrances and Exits". I defended my Ph.D. proposal in October 2018, and plan to graduate with a doctoral degree in Summer 2019.
