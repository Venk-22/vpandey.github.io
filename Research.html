Research


Topics of Interest

  1. Large-scale long-term planning models for complex driver route choice behavior under the presence of emerging technologies
  2. Models for multi-company competition in transportation service market and policy design
  3. Dynamic stochastic control of Intelligent Transportation Systems including active traffic management (ATM) strategies, dynamic pricing for managed lanes, signal coordination and optimization, etc.
  4. Data-driven analysis for calibrating driver behavior and planning models
