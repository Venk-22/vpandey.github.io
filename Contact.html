For Prospective Students

If you're considering joining our lab, please note the following:

  • Submit your application to the graduate programs at North Carolina A&T State University.
  • After applying, you may reach out to me via email. However, please understand that due to the high volume of emails I receive, I may not be able to respond to every inquiry.
  • The primary quality I look for in students is genuine motivation and passion for research. If you have undertaken projects or activities that demonstrate exceptional dedication to your field, be sure to highlight these in your communication.
  • If you believe our research interests align closely, feel free to send an email detailing this alignment. However, please note that a response is not guaranteed.
  • Stand-out candidates often have a unique story or achievement that showcases their commitment to the transportation, community planning, and emerging technologies.
